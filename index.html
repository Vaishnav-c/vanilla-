VANILLA ICE CREAM

Vanillais freaquently used to flavour ice cream, especially in North america,Asia and Europe.

INGREDIANTS

  • vanilla essence
  • chilled heavy cream
  • condensed milk

Making Process

Step 1: Chill the bowl and whisk.
In fridge for 30 min
Step 2: Whipping or heavy cream.
2 cup
Step 3: Beat until stif peaks form.
step 4: Whipped cream will fluffy like this
step 5: Condensed milk
1 cup
step 6: Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
step 7: mix well
step 8: Pour the mixture into a mould
step 9: Cover the mould with wrapping paper
step 10: Keep it in fridge for 6 hours
Or over night
step 11: Remove the wrapping paper
step 12: Now scoop out the ice cream
>
step 13:Now the vanilla ice cream is ready
